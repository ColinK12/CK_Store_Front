From Our Small Start

While still in his teens, my grandfather sought out American Bonsai masters like John Naka and Ben Oki renowned artists who taught him their bonsai concepts and techniques, and helped refine his skills. As his talents grew, so did the bonsai collection in the family’s backyard. Soon he was selling small trees to make room for more. At the beginning of 1974 a fledgling Bonz-Ey Garden had sprouted.

Today, 46 years later, we continue to grow bonsai, and ship world wide. Continuing the tradition my grandfather started in his backyard. We believe in environmentaly friendly practices, and excellent customer service.
